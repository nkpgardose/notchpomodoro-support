Notch Pomodoro — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Notch Pomodoro · Last updated 17 July 2026

Notch Pomodoro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app is fully offline. It has no account, no sign-in, no network connection, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

What the app stores

Everything the app records stays on your Mac:

  • Your interval settings (durations, colors, completion sound) and your Pomodoro session history are saved in a local database on your device.
  • Daily Markdown summaries are written only to a folder you explicitly choose, and only when you ask to export them.

None of this information ever leaves your Mac, and it is never sent to us or to anyone else.

Data we collect

None. Notch Pomodoro collects no data of any kind. Because there is no network access and no analytics, we have no way to see how you use the app, and we receive nothing from it.

Third parties

The app uses no advertising networks, no analytics services, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
